# Mata App - Feature Backlog

Suggested new features to add value to the app.

---

## üöÄ High-Impact Features

### 1. Bundle Swaps / Multi-Item Trades

Allow users to offer multiple items in a single swap deal (e.g., trade 3 smaller items for 1 larger item).

**Value**: Increases deal flexibility and swap success rates.

---

### 2. Swap Value Indicator

AI-powered feature suggesting approximate "swap value" based on category, condition, and market trends.

**Value**: Helps users understand fair trades without involving money.

---

### 3. Community / Groups

Location-based or interest-based swap communities (e.g., "Vintage Collectors Auckland", "Kids Stuff Exchange").

**Value**: Targeted visibility and stronger community engagement.

---

### 4. Scheduled Meetup Integration

Built-in scheduling for item exchanges:

- Calendar integration
- Suggested safe public meeting spots
- Meetup confirmation/reminders
- Safety check-in feature

**Value**: Safer, more organized exchanges.

---

### 5. Item Verification Badges

Verified items reviewed via video/photos get a trust badge.

**Value**: Increases buyer confidence and reduces disputes.

---

### 6. Swap Stories / Success Feed

Social feed showing completed swaps (with user consent) to inspire users.

**Value**: Builds community trust and engagement.

---

## üîß Utility Features

### 7. Saved Searches with Alerts

Save search criteria and receive push notifications when matching items are posted.

**Value**: Improves item discovery and user retention.

---

### 8. Item Request Board

"Looking for" posts where users describe items they want. Others can respond with matching items.

**Value**: Connects supply with demand proactively.

---

### 9. Barcode / Product Scanner

Scan product barcodes to auto-fill item details using a product database.

**Value**: Faster item listing, better data quality.

---

### 10. Swap Statistics Dashboard

Personal analytics:

- Items swapped, given away, received
- Environmental impact (items saved from landfill)
- Community leaderboard

**Value**: Gamification and environmental awareness.

---

### 11. User Verification Levels

Tiered trust badges:

- Email verified
- Phone verified
- ID verified
- Trusted Swapper (many successful swaps)

**Value**: Increased platform trust.

---

### 12. Escrow-Style Swap Confirmation

Both parties must confirm receipt before deal is marked complete.

**Value**: Reduces disputes and scams.

---

### 13. Video Chat for Items

In-app video call to show items in real-time before committing.

**Value**: Transparency and reduced post-swap issues.

---
